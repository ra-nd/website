Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to provide cleaning supplies or a vacuum?

No, we come fully prepared! Our team brings professional-grade cleaning solutions, fresh microfiber cloths, and high-performance HEPA-filter vacuums to every job. If you have specific products you prefer us to use, just let us know.

Are you pet-friendly and safe for my home?

Absolutely. We love pets! We use eco-friendly and non-toxic cleaning agents that are safe for both your furry friends and children. We also ensure our staff are trained to be respectful of your pets' space while we work.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

It is entirely up to you. Many of our clients provide us with a key or a lockbox code so we can clean while they are at work. All our staff are fully vetted and background-checked for your peace of mind.

What areas do you cover in Fife and Tayside?

We currently serve Dundee City, St Andrews, Tayport, Newport-on-Tay, Cupar, and most of East Fife. If you aren't sure if we cover your location, give us a call and we'll let you know!

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that life happens. We ask for at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your booking. This allows us to reallocate the time slot to another client.
